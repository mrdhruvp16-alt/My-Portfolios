Crafted in the Shadows of Grasse

Since 1987, our master perfumers have retreated to a small stone atelier in the hills above Grasse. Here, they pursue a singular obsession: the poetry of darkness rendered in scent. No shortcuts. No synthetics. Only the finest raw materials from seven continents.

The Ingredients

Where Rarity Meets Craft

Black Rose
Turkey · Halfeti

The rarest rose on earth, blooming only along a single stretch of the Euphrates river.

Cambodia · Wild
